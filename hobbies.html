Hobbies

Good job on finding this one! These are the hobbies I'm currently pursuing as of '24. Some of them are wild, I'd like to say. Have fun perusing this page!

Ice Skating

This one surprised me. Most recently, I've picked up ice skating as a result of being Miyagi'd during an impulse stop at the WEM Ice Palace. Just gotta figure out how to stop now!

Sewing

Being this size means a lot of clothes get worn down quite faster than expected. I picked up a needle for a Home Economics class back in fifth grade, and while I never completely remembered everything, I still try.

Working Out

What a surprise, right? I paid some schmuck a total of a thousand dollars, I believe, for an entire six months worth of personal training in the gym I go to. Was it worth it? Probably not. But it did put me in the right direction and mindset on how I would go about doing things in the gym. Do I follow it all the time? No. :)

Biking

I've learned to bike when I was 15. Quite late, you'd imagine. But it's never too late to learn. Some of the rips on my jacket happened because I wiped out on my bike. It's fun pretending you're in a music video and suddenly snapping the front wheel away from the road.

Games

Do you even have to ask? The first game I remember ever playing was Jet Set Radio Future on the original Xbox. It was a hell of a time, not knowing how to play a game and wondering how I kept losing. Probably also what started my anger issues. From then, it was a back-and-forth between PCs from computer cafés and whatever console was 'borrowed' by my family.
