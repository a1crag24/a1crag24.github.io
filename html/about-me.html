Who am I?

I am a1crag24, also known as Reynel. If you're in NAIT since '23, you've probably seen a fat kid in a blue and white jacket.

That's me! Don't be afraid to say hello, because I am!

As of 2024, I'm attending as a Game Programming student in the first year. I figured to get back into school again to put myself to work onto something I'm even half-interested in so I could see if it is something I want to do.

Even as a kid, I've had delusions of making games ever since I was in the 3rd grade. Didn't act on it until the 6th grade, where I started making Minecraft Pocket Editions mods on JavaScript. Yes, I have too many grey hairs for my age.

I got more experience from learning HTML later on around tne 7th grade which helped me cement what you see around the site right now.

Other than those main ones, most of my experience has purely been theoretical: game farming scripts, more game mods, little things.

I may know a lot of things, but I'm not an expert yet! As they say, "jack of all trades, master of none, but better than a master of one".
